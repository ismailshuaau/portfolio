MentorLive

MentorLive is an automated mentor matchmaking platform for Entrepreneurs and SMEs.

Where?

Hack the Crisis

Why?

In times of crisis when the situation requires timely and critical decisions, it's important to have a fast way to find relevant mentors for guidance. Hence costly mistakes can be avoided.

How does it work?

  • Select the type of user
  • Fill in your profile
  • Select a match from the result

Tech

Bootstrap for front and Laravel framwork for backend.
